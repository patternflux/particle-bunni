## Organic Forms & Sonic Shapes Vol. 2 has landed

<iframe style="border: 0; width: 640px; height: 760px;" src="https://bandcamp.com/EmbeddedPlayer/album=2051205436/size=large/bgcol=ffffff/linkcol=0687f5/tracklist=false/transparent=true/" seamless><a href="https://particlebunni.bandcamp.com/album/organic-forms-sonic-shapes-vol-2">Organic Forms &amp; Sonic Shapes Vol.2 by Particle Bunni</a></iframe>

As promised, we return with a continued exploration of sound and moments.

We both stepped away from the working week that drains the soul and stifles creativity — and in that space, the floodgates opened. What followed was a motherlode of new pieces. While still exploring the same terrain as Volume 1, this time we had the freedom, time, and openness to discover entirely new sonic continents. The result feels richer, deeper, and more expansive.

We hope you hear it. We hope you feel it.
Enjoy the blossoming — there is much more to come.

Looking ahead, 2026 feels like it’s shaping up to be an enlightening year.

We’ve had feedback that our material would work beautifully for film soundtracks and television — and we agree. We’re open to conversations around licensing, commissions, and creative collaborations. We also believe this work would translate powerfully into dance and theatre productions.

See you soon — and enjoy the music.

— Particle Bunni
