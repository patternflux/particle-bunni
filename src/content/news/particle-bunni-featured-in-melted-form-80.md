Particle Bunni was featured in Issue #80 of Melted Form, the Substack newsletter by Spencer Cowley focused on ambient and experimental electronic music.

Melted Form highlights independent artists working in texture, repetition, and exploratory sound. We've been reading the newsletter and following the community around it for a while, so it means a lot to be included.

Organic Forms & Sonic Shapes Vol. 2 was mentioned in the issue alongside other artists operating in the ambient electronic space.

You can read the full issue here:
[Melted Form #80: Survival Instincts](https://meltedform.substack.com/p/80-survival-instincts)

Organic Forms & Sonic Shapes Vol. 2 is available on Bandcamp and all streaming platforms.
