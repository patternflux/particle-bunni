Particle Bunni is an ambient-electronic collaboration between multidisciplinary artists Terrance Stearns and Grayson West.

Blending electronic sound generation, guitar, analog instruments, voice, and field recordings, the project explores the intersection of mysticism, psychoacoustics, and digital folklore.

Inspired by ritual sound practices, particle physics, and post-industrial dreamscapes, Particle Bunni transforms stillness into signal and silence into texture.

Based in Seattle, WA, the duo's work extends through the Discoastal Culture Collective, a growing network of experimental musicians, poets, and visual artists building bridges between digital and physical worlds.
